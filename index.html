Your 9-Week Journey To BFSI Readiness

A weekly breakdown of the intensive, hands-on skills you will master to become a top-tier BFSI Sales professional.

Week 1

Sales Mindset & Finance Basics

  • Grit & Resilience: Handling Rejection, Sales Ethics in BFSI.
  • Compensation: Understanding Fixed vs. Variable Pay, Quota, and Commission.
  • Output: Create a personal P&L tracker.
    • Week 2

    ICP, Prospecting & Sales Funnel

    • Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) & Lead Sourcing (LinkedIn, local databases).
    • Sales Funnel: Basics of Lead, Prospect, Opportunity, and Customer.
  • Output: Build a qualified list of 50 target leads.
    • Week 3

    BANT Qualification Deep Dive

    • Mastering BANT for financial services (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline).
    • Pre-Call Research (Competitors, Credit Health, Regulatory News).
  • Output: Develop BANT scorecards for 5 qualified prospects.
    • Week 4

    Cold Calling & Email Mastery

    • Cold Calling Mastery: Opening statements, gatekeeper navigation.
    • Cold Emailing: Crafting compelling subject lines (AIDA) & DND compliance.
  • Output: 5 recorded Qualification Calls & draft a 3-email sequence.
    • Week 5

    SPIN Selling: Situation & Problem

    • Questioning Techniques: Mastering the Situation & Problem stages.
    • Active Listening: Techniques for mirroring, summarizing, and showing empathy.
  • Output: Role-play a discovery call focusing on problem identification.
    • Week 6

    SPIN Selling: Implication & Need-Payoff

    • Mastering Implication & Need-Payoff questions.
    • Pitching Value, Not Features: Structuring the presentation for ROI.
  • Output: Full 10-minute value-focused Product Presentation.
    • Week 7

    Objection Handling & Closing Skills

    • Anticipating Objections: Top 10 BFSI objections.
    • Handling Techniques: Feel-Felt-Found, Deflection, and Clarification.
  • Output: High-stakes, graded Negotiation role-play.
    • Week 8

    CRM Practical & Forecasting

    • CRM Practical: Hands-on Sandbox training (logging activities, updating stages).
    • Forecasting & Sales Reporting: Accurately predicting deal closure.
  • Output: Comprehensive CRM Pipeline Audit/Sales forecast submission.
    • Week 9

    Industry Immersion & Placement Prep

    • Live projects with industry partners & Resume building.
    • Mock interviews with industry experts & Placement drives.
  • Output: Portfolio submission and first-round placement interviews.
